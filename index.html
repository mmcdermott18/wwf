our process

We begin by carefully selecting the freshest, in-season flowers sourced from nearby gardens and farms. Each bloom is picked for its unique character and beauty, ensuring that every arrangement tells a story. We then transform these natural treasures into enchanting compositions, weaving together colors, textures, and fragrances that evoke emotions and memories. The result is a harmonious symphony of blooms that not only graces your special day with its elegance but also champions sustainability and a connection to our surroundings. With every arrangement, we strive to create an unforgettable experience, where the magic of local, in-season flowers becomes an integral part of your wedding journey.
